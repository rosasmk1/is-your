    About Is Yours

    Our solution:

    We are a scalable digital platform based on emerging technological solutions that, through mobile devices, connect companies with people that are seeking a service and/or vacancy with immigrants, diverse and inclusive groups.

    Problem:

    We want to make the workforce visible in its majority Migrant, Diversity and inclusion groups; By connecting occupations with opportunities through an agile digital platform, allowing them to find a job in the hotel, gastronomy - bar and catering sector.

    Diversity, inclusion, fairness, pillars of sustainability, corporate social responsibility; a central issue on the agendas of the organizational world.
